PWA Icon Generation Instructions
=================================

To complete your PWA setup, you need to generate icons for different sizes.

You can use these free tools:

1. PWA Asset Generator (Recommended):
   https://www.pwabuilder.com/imageGenerator
   - Upload a 512x512px image
   - It will generate all required sizes automatically

2. Favicon.io:
   https://favicon.io/
   - Generate from text, image, or emoji

3. RealFaviconGenerator:
   https://realfavicongenerator.net/
   - Upload your logo
   - Generates all PWA icons

Required Icon Sizes for PWA:
- 72x72px (icon-72x72.png)
- 96x96px (icon-96x96.png)
- 128x128px (icon-128x128.png)
- 144x144px (icon-144x144.png)
- 152x152px (icon-152x152.png)
- 192x192px (icon-192x192.png)
- 384x384px (icon-384x384.png)
- 512x512px (icon-512x512.png)

Design Suggestion:
Create a simple icon with a golden diamond/gem symbol on an amber background
to match your app's theme.

Once generated, place all PNG files in the /public/icons/ directory.
